A Sanctuary for
Slow Mornings.

Cafflora began in a sun-drenched corner of a quiet street, born from the belief that coffee and flowers are the two simplest ways to cultivate joy.

We curate our space to be an extension of your own living room—a botanical atelier where the scent of heirloom roses mingles with artisanal roasts. Every bloom is hand-selected, and every cup is poured with intention.
