Our Story

Where Art Meets the Earth

Flora began as a small dream in a Parisian corner—a vision to create a space that nourishes both the palate and the spirit. We believe that a perfect cup of coffee is the start of a story, and a single bloom is a poem waiting to be read.

Every bean is ethically sourced, every petal is hand-picked, and every book on our shelves is chosen to inspire your next great adventure.
